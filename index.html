River City Retro

River City Retro was founded in 2014 and previously known as "MilliganGames". River City Retro is owned and operated by Patrick Milligan in Oswego, New York.

River City Retro is an online store specializing in classic video games, like Atari, NES, Super Nintendo, Nintendo 64, Game Boy, Sega Genesis, and more.

Patrick has been selling video games and other collectibles online since 2008. Prompt shipping and friendly customer service are our trademarks.
